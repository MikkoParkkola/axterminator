# Commercial Use

AXTerminator is free for personal, research, educational, noncommercial
open-source, and free public-good projects with attribution.

Business use requires a written commercial license.

Examples that require a commercial license:

- A company uses AXTerminator internally for agent workflows, testing,
  automation, demos, or operations.
- AXTerminator is embedded in a paid app, SDK, agent platform, SaaS, or
  consulting deliverable.
- AXTerminator materially improves a product, service, or workflow that creates
  revenue or business value.
- A commercial organization uses AXTerminator through a fork, wrapper, hosted
  service, or private integration.

Commercial terms are flexible and can be structured as:

- Attribution plus upstream collaboration.
- Revenue share.
- Fixed license fee.
- Support or integration contract.
- Strategic partnership.

Commercial users should contact Mikko Parkkola before shipping or relying on
AXTerminator in a business context.
